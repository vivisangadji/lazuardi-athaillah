Welcome to
Lazuardi Athaillah Global Compassionate School

Lazuardi Athaillah merupakan cabang ke-tiga belas dari jaringan Sekolah Islam Lazuardi yang berpusat di Cinere, Depok. Mengusung semangat pendidikan yang sama, Sekolah Islam Lazuardi Athaillah menggaungkan karakter insan mulia, mengedapankan nilai-nilai Ke-Islaman dan menyiapkan generasi yang memiliki wawasan serta kecakapan global.

ABOUT US

Our Mission

The main mission of Lazuardi Athaillah is to inspire and guide students to reach their full potential through the dedication and enthusiasm of our teachers. As a globally certified school, we collaborate internationally to integrate best practices in education to enhance students' learning experiences. We provide various flagship programs tailored to empower students with specialized learning experiences and growth opportunities. Additionally, we prioritize student support services and mentoring to ensure every student receives the personalized guidance and assistance they need.

  • Elementary School educates students with strong Islamic values, comprehensive academic knowledge, and noble character.
  • Junior High School nurtures students with robust Islamic values, extensive academic knowledge, and exemplary character.
  • Hybrid Learning program allows students to learn anywhere, combining the flexibility of online education with the structure of in-person instruction.

Our Benefits

Lazuardi Athaillah offers various benefits to parents and prospective students, including high-quality education with international standards, dedicated teachers, and global collaboration that opens international opportunities. The school provides personalized support and mentoring, flagship programs to develop talents and interests, and an environment that fosters character development and Islamic values. Additionally, modern facilities, an innovative curriculum, and counseling services ensure that students receive the best learning experiences and the academic and emotional support they need.
